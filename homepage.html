The Tinytan Characters



Hyung Line

Members: Jin, Suga, J-Hope, and RM. Traits: The hyung line consists of the older members. They tend to be more mature, responsible, and sometimes quieter. Fun Fact: If they’re being noisy and talkative while crossing over a puddle, it’s likely the hyung line!

Some Info About Them

RM (Kim Nam Joon):Birthdate: September 12, 1994.Nickname: Formerly known as Rap Monster, he changed his stage name to RM, which stands for “Real Me.” Jin (Kim Seok Jin):Birthdate: December 4, 1992.Cooking Enthusiast: Loves cooking and taking selcas. Suga (Min Yoon Gi):Birthdate: March 9, 1993.Stage Name Origin: Derived from “shooting guard” (his position in basketball) and “sugar.” J-Hope (Jung Ho Seok):Birthdate: February 18, 1994.Dance Machine: Known for his powerful dance moves.



Maknae Line

Members: Jimin, V, and Jungkook. Traits: The maknae line includes the youngest members. They are often playful, energetic, and expressive. Fun Fact: When introducing a new song, if they start dancing, it’s probably the maknae line!

Some Info About Them

Jungkook (Golden Maknae): Nickname: Jungkook is often called the “Golden Maknae.” This title reflects his versatility—he excels in various areas. Birthdate: September 1, 1997. V (Taehyung):Birthdate: December 30, 1995. Background: V was born in Daegu and later moved to Seoul.
